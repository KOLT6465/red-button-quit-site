Privacy Policy

Red Button Quit does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

Data collection

Red Button Quit has no accounts, analytics, advertising, tracking, cloud services, or network communication. We do not receive any data from the app.

Mouse events

The app observes left mouse clicks and non-content window metadata, such as window position, only to recognize an intentional click on an enabled app's red close button. It never reads window contents. Event coordinates and window metadata are processed briefly on your Mac and are never logged, stored, or transmitted.

Settings stored on your Mac

Your enabled preference, launch-at-login preference, and per-app choices remain on your Mac. Per-app choices contain only app display names and bundle identifiers. Red Button Quit does not retain application paths, document access, or security-scoped bookmarks. macOS manages the app's permission status.

Third parties

The app contains no third-party SDKs and does not share data with anyone.

Children's privacy

Red Button Quit does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Policy changes

If the app's privacy practices change, this policy will be updated before the changed version is released. The effective date above identifies the current version.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to koltin@heapsea.com.
